Welcome to Bourbon Warfare


At Bourbon Warfare, we follow the game-play frame of mind of "Accessible
Realism." We define accessible realism as the ability of a casual gamer
to competently and confidently take part in our sessions without the
need for extensive training sessions. This is achieved through a brief
SOP (Standard Operating Procedure) manual which acts as a guide for how
we conduct actions in game; including basic tactics, formations,
terminology, and proper communication techniques.
